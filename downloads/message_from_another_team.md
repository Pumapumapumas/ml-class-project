Hi All,

I put together a Telugu OCR dataset on HuggingFace that everyone is welcome to use to start testing out their project.

The corpus contains ~221 digitized Telugu books (~13GB) with paired page images (.jpg) and transcribed text (.txt) spanning historical and literary texts.

I put this together independently, so use it at your own risk. Once an official corpus is available for the course, consider deferring to that instead.

https://huggingface.co/datasets/AlbertoChestnut/telugu-ocr
